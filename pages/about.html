About Classroom List

Classroom List is a simple web application that allows you to keep track of various classrooms and their details. You can add new classrooms, search for specific classrooms, and view all the existing classrooms in the list.

It is designed with ease of use in mind, and it's a great tool for teachers, students, or anyone involved in managing classroom information.
